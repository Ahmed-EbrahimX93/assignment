About Us

Milaf... The Trademark of Quality

At Milaf, our passion for excellence drives us to continually elevate the quality of our dates and extend their reach across the globe. Inspired by the visionary " Al Madinah Heritage Company," a proud entity of Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund, we are on a mission to present this treasured product in a way that honors the Kingdom's rich cultural legacy. Our dates aren't just a treat—they're a symbol of our economic pride and a testament to the timeless traditions embedded in our national identity. Milaf is more than a brand; it's an enduring emblem of Saudi hospitality, rooted in heritage and destined to be shared worldwide.

 

From the Palm’s First Roots, Our Journey Began

Our proud history is bathed in the gentle embrace of the palm, and our thriving present is crowned by its majestic stature. The palm tree, a beacon of pride and prosperity in our national emblem, gives us its rich fruits as a symbol of our boundless generosity.
Our dates awaken memories of warmth and closeness, infusing our lives with joy and celebration. From this legacy, "Milaf" was born—to revolutionize the future of Saudi dates and share their nutritional riches with the world.
 

Our Dates

At Milaf, we take pride in nurturing and sharing the rich flavors of Saudi dates. With a deep commitment to quality, we carefully select and craft our products, following a seamless process from farm to table. From the moment our dates are harvested using the latest techniques, to the final stages of production, every step is infused with care. We ensure that only the finest dates, reflecting our passion and dedication, reach your hands—bringing a taste of Saudi excellence to the world.
